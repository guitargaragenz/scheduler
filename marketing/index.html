About Us

A Workshop Built for Musicians

Guitar Garage NZ Ltd is a specialist repair workshop tucked away at 12 Victory St, Welcome Bay, Tauranga — run by Trevor Collings, a passionate technician with deep experience across guitars, amplifiers, and studio electronics.

Whether your vintage valve amp has gone quiet, your Floyd Rose needs a full setup, or your synth has developed a mind of its own, Trevor brings the same care and expertise to every job. No job too small, no gear too beloved.

Why Musicians Trust Us

  • Specialist knowledge, not generalist guesswork
  • Honest assessments — free of charge
  • All work explained clearly before we start
  • Tauranga-based, locally trusted
Services

Everything Your Gear Needs

From a simple setup to a complex electronics repair, Guitar Garage NZ handles it all — with care, precision, and a genuine love of great-sounding gear.

Guitar Setup & Maintenance

Full setups for acoustic and electric guitars, including tremolo and Floyd Rose systems. Action, Intonation, tuning stability — dialled in properly.

Guitar Repairs

Fret levelling and crowning, nut replacement, wiring and electronics, hardware installation and upgrades. Your guitar restored to its best.

Amplifier Servicing & Repair

Valve, solid state, and modelling amplifiers. From re-tubing and bias setting to full fault diagnosis and repair — all amp types welcomed.

Keyboard & Synthesizer Repair

Faulty keys, dead oscillators, connectivity issues — Trevor services and repairs a wide range of keyboards and synthesizers to get your sounds flowing again.

PA System Servicing

Keep your live sound performing at its best. Guitar Garage NZ services and repairs PA systems — from speaker cabinets to power amplifiers and mixers.

Studio Equipment Repair

Audio interfaces, outboard gear, studio monitors, and more. Precision repairs for the equipment that underpins your recording sessions.

What We Work On

All Brands, All Eras, All Types

From a battered 1970s Strat to a modern modelling amp or a classic Rhodes — if it makes music, Trevor can likely fix it. Guitar Garage NZ works across a wide range of brands and instrument types.

♪ Electric Guitars

Fender, Gibson, PRS, Ibanez, Gretsch, and everything in between — including vintage and boutique instruments.

♪ Acoustic Guitars

Steel-string and classical acoustics, including setup, bridge saddle work, and nut replacements.

♪ Amplifiers

Marshall, Fender, Vox, Mesa Boogie, Roland, Line 6 — valve, solid state, and digital modellers.

♩ Keyboards & Synths

Vintage analogue synths, digital workstations, stage pianos — repaired and serviced with care.

How It Works

Simple. Straightforward. Sorted.

Getting your gear repaired shouldn't be complicated. Here's how Guitar Garage NZ makes it easy from start to finish.

1. Book Your Free Assessment

Use the online booking tool to schedule a free, no-obligation assessment at a time that suits you.

2. Drop Off Your Gear

Bring your instrument or equipment to the workshop at 12 Victory St, Welcome Bay. Trevor will look it over and talk you through what's needed.

3. Collect Once It's Sorted

You'll be notified when your gear is ready. Pick it up knowing it's been serviced with care and backed by solid workmanship.
