# Guitar Setup

More often than not, a new **Guitar** or **Bass** will only have had a basic **setup**, if at all. When not setup properly a guitar can be **difficult to tune** **& play** and have a negative affect on the **tone** it could potentially produce. Humidity, temperature, wear and string gauge changes can also require the need for further maintenance.

**We can set up, repair or modify your Guitar or Bass to your playing style to ensure it plays, feels and sounds the best it can.**

**Re-Strings, Action, Intonation, Fretwork, Bone Nuts, Electrics & Repairs** for individuals, **Schools**, **Music Centres.**
