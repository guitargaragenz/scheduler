Resurrecting guitars from the dead

I’m Harry, your local guitar wizard and luthier based out of Hamilton. With years of experience in guitar repair and maintenance, I specialize in extended range and drop tuned guitars and basses, but am no stranger to the regular 6 string electric and acoustic guitars. Whether your guitar has a scratchy pot and a noisy input jack, or your strings are buzzing all over the fretboard because your frets need some love, bring your guitar in to get it playing like new again.

Services Provided

I provide all luthier services necessary to keep your instrument in top shape. If you would like to discuss a service not featured here, send me a message or give me a call.

Restringing

Keeping your guitar strings fresh is crucial for maintaining its sound quality and playability. I offer professional restringing services to ensure your guitar sounds its best.

Setups

A proper setup can make a world of difference in how your guitar feels and plays. My setup services include adjusting the truss rod, nut and bridge, setting the action, and intonating the guitar.

Electronic Work

From pickup replacements to complete rewiring, I handle all aspects of electric guitar work. If you’re experiencing issues with your guitar’s electronics, I can diagnose and fix the problem.

Fretwork

Over time, frets can wear down, causing buzzing and intonation issues. I provide comprehensive fretwork services, including fret leveling, crowning, and polishing.
