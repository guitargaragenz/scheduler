About Calico Guitarworks

About
Calico Guitarworks

At Calico Guitarworks, our goal is simple: to help musicians spend less time worrying about their instruments and more time making music. In addition to being Long Island’s premier destination for fretted musical instrument repair, we design and build handcrafted electric and acoustic guitars and, through our GuitarRepairParts.com brand, provide a trusted source for parts and repair supplies worldwide. 

Our team is committed to providing quick, honest, and reliable service. With more than 30 years of combined professional guitar repair experience, we bring a deep understanding of instrument care, restoration, and performance to everything we do. Built on craftsmanship, guided by experience, and grounded in exceptional service, Calico Guitarworks helps musicians get the very best from their instruments.
